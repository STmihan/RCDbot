Home Terms of Service

1. Data We Collect

  • Server IDs & Channel IDs: to know where to create and delete voice rooms.
  • User IDs: only while users are inside a temporary voice room.
  • Configuration Settings: the options you set for your server.

2. How We Use Data

Data is used exclusively to provide the bot’s core functionality. No data is sold or shared with third parties.

3. Data Retention

Configuration data persists until you remove the bot or run a “reset” command. Voice-presence data is deleted immediately after channels are removed.

4. Your Rights

You may request deletion of configuration data at any time by contacting the maintainer or running the built-in reset.
